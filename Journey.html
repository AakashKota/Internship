THE ROOTS

Our entire life when we shop, we are limited by choices that are there in a store. We are often on the side where we buy our favourite coloured shoes made by
someone else but what if we designed it? What if we could involve in the making of a shoe we want to buy? This little thought sprouted to become what we are
today, KICKSUPI :)
